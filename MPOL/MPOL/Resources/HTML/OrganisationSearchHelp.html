General Conditions

Organisations and Businesses can be searched by name, ABN or ACN number.

  • Alpha numeric and special characters are allowed.
  • Search is NOT case sensitive.

Exact Match

  • Enclose your search string with quotation marks ("") to find an exact match.

eg. "Orion Central Bank" will only return Orion Central Bank and NOT orion Bank.

Wildcard Search

Supported for ABN and ACN numbers.

  • Use a (?) to represent one unknown character.

eg. 1004678232?

  • Use a (*) to represent multiple unknown characters.

eg. 1004678*
